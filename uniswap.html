The Problem Students Face

Buying and selling used student items is still messy, unsafe, and time-consuming.

😤

WhatsApp Chaos

Students waste hours in unorganized group chats trying to find or sell second-hand items.

💸

No Fair Pricing

There is no clear price reference, so buyers may overpay and sellers may underprice.

🔒

Low Safety

No proper verification, ratings, or protection from scams, fake sellers, and no-shows.

Why not just use WhatsApp?

WhatsApp groups are useful for quick messages, but they are not built for student trading. Items get buried, prices are hard to compare, sellers are difficult to verify, and students often need to share personal phone numbers.

UniSwap BH solves this by adding verified student accounts, organized categories, item search, ratings, safer campus meetup points, and in-app chat — all in one focused marketplace.
